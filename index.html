About Mystic Live

Mystic Live is a dynamic Sri Lankan band that merges the rich textures of traditional Sri Lankan music with the driving energy of Western pop rock and indie. Their distinctive sound blends indigenous rhythms and melodies with modern guitar riffs and compelling lyrics, creating a unique musical experience that bridges cultural worlds. Mystic's performances are a vibrant celebration of both local heritage and contemporary influences, offering a fresh and engaging take on global and local music traditions
